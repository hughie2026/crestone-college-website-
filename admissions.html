Admissions

Apply with a question, a record of effort, and a reason to care.

The College welcomes students, early researchers, professionals, visiting scholars, and interdisciplinary practitioners whose goals align with public-purpose research.

Who should apply

Curious, self-directed, ethically attentive learners.

Strong applicants demonstrate readiness for research-led study, intellectual openness across disciplines, and the ability to connect technical or academic work with human and public consequences.

01Select an academic pathway.
02Prepare academic or professional records.
03Write a statement of research interest.
04Submit a writing sample or project portfolio.
05Complete an interview when requested.

A focused admissions process

Step 1

Pathway selection

Identify the undergraduate- or graduate-level diploma that best matches your goals and preparation.

Step 2

Academic review

The College reviews research fit, readiness, ethical awareness, and interdisciplinary potential.

Step 3

Enrollment planning

Admitted learners receive guidance on studios, requirements, research expectations, and the academic calendar.
