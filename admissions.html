Admissions

Join a small research community built around real-world questions.

The Crestone College welcomes students, early researchers, professionals, visiting scholars, and interdisciplinary practitioners interested in public-purpose research.

Who Should Apply

Applicants with research curiosity and public purpose.

Prospective students should demonstrate intellectual curiosity, ethical awareness, interdisciplinary interest, and a commitment to applying research to health, technology, environment, or social challenges.

The College’s low-residency and hybrid model is designed for learners who can engage in research workshops, independent study, collaborative inquiry, and applied projects.

Application Materials

  • Online application form
  • Academic records or professional background
  • Statement of research interest
  • Writing sample or project portfolio
  • Interview when required

Admissions Process

A focused review process.

01

Choose a Pathway

Select the undergraduate or graduate diploma pathway that fits your academic and professional goals.

02

Prepare Materials

Submit records, a research interest statement, and relevant writing or project examples.

03

Academic Review

Applications are reviewed for research fit, readiness, ethical awareness, and interdisciplinary potential.

04

Enrollment Planning

Accepted students receive guidance on program structure, research studios, and academic expectations.
