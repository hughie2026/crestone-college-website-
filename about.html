About the college

A deliberately small institution with a wide field of view.

The Crestone College draws on the intellectual tradition of Crestone Lab, established in 1989, and translates that experimental culture into a formal research and education model.

01

The institutional idea

The College concentrates resources on inquiry, teaching, collaboration, and translation rather than on layers of schools, departments, and administration.

Its operating model is anchored in three interdisciplinary Academic Hubs and an Office of Academic & Administrative Services. The result is a structure intended to remain close to the work: research teams, students, partners, and the public questions they share.

Denver as a research setting.
Health, technology, energy, environmental change, public policy, and the transformation of communities in the American West.
02

From Crestone Lab to The Crestone College

The origin story is less about institutional expansion than about preserving a culture of independent, high-density research.

1989

Crestone Lab begins

An independent research platform is established around human development, scientific exploration, environmental change, and knowledge innovation.

Development

An open research culture

Researchers, educators, engineers, thinkers, and social practitioners work across conventional boundaries and toward practical public value.

Today

A formal college model

The College translates that culture into Academic Hubs, research-led education, lean governance, and external collaboration.
