Founding President

Dr. Adrian Keller, Ph.D.

Medical AI & Human Systems Researcher

Dr. Adrian Keller is the Founding President of The Crestone College and a research-driven leader working at the intersection of medical AI and human health systems. His research focuses on medical image analysis, predictive health risk modeling, personalized intervention, AI clinical decision support, and the ethical governance of medical AI.

Before founding The Crestone College, Dr. Keller served as principal investigator, strategic advisor, and program lead across multiple medical technology, health data, and interdisciplinary research projects. He is especially interested in how small, high-density research institutions can connect medicine, AI, and social impact through lean organizations, cross-domain labs, and rapid translation.

As Founding President, Dr. Keller leads the college’s research architecture, academic direction, and international partnerships, anchored in three Academic Hubs: Health Sciences & Human Performance; AI, Data & Society; and Environment, Resilience & Human Futures.
