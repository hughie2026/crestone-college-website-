Leadership & governance

Lean governance, visible responsibility.

The College uses a compact structure designed to keep academic standards, institutional risk, operations, and research quality clearly accountable.

Founding president

Dr. Adrian Keller, Ph.D.

Medical AI & Human Systems Researcher

Dr. Keller leads the College's institutional strategy, academic direction, research architecture, external partnerships, international framework, and public representation.

His research interests include medical imaging intelligence, predictive health-risk assessment, personalized intervention systems, AI-supported clinical decisions, public-health modeling, and the ethical governance of medical AI.
