Founding President

Dr. Adrian Keller, Ph.D.

Founding President, The Crestone College

Dr. Adrian Keller is the Founding President of The Crestone College and a research leader focused on medical artificial intelligence and human health systems.

His research interests include medical imaging intelligence, predictive health risk assessment, personalized intervention systems, AI-supported clinical decision making, public health modeling, and the ethical governance of medical AI.
