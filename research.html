Research

The College is organized by fields of inquiry.

Each Academic Hub combines a clear intellectual identity with an obligation to work across boundaries when public problems demand it.

Hub I

Health Sciences & Human Performance

The hub studies health, wellbeing, public health, prevention, performance, recovery, digital health, longevity, and personalized intervention.

PlatformCenter for Health, Wellbeing & Human Performance
QuestionHow can people sustain health, resilience, and quality of life amid rapid change?
FacultyFive core and research faculty profiles.

View Hub I faculty

Research themes include preventive health, mental wellbeing, digital health tools, health behavior, and human performance assessment.
Technical research is paired with governance, ethics, human-centered design, and public responsibility.

Hub II

AI, Data & Society

The hub studies AI and data systems as technical, institutional, ethical, and social forces shaping health, education, work, governance, and public life.

PlatformApplied AI & Data Lab
QuestionHow can AI and data systems serve society in responsible, trustworthy, human-centered ways?

Hub III

Environment, Resilience & Human Futures

The hub uses Colorado and the American West as important contexts for climate resilience, water and land systems, sustainable cities, community adaptation, and futures research.
