Founding president

Dr. Adrian Keller, Ph.D.

Medical AI and human systems researcher; architect of the College's compact interdisciplinary model.

Dr. Keller's leadership joins scientific rigor, responsible technology, and public-purpose research. His work focuses on medical imaging intelligence, clinical data science, predictive health, human performance, and the ethical governance of AI in health systems.
